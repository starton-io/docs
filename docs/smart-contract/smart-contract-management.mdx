# Smart contract management

The Smart contract management is an abstraction on top of our [Transaction Manager](/docs/transactions/transaction-management) and it allows you to build and **automate** your smart contract transactions easily, without struggling with [ABI](/guides/ABI) and parameters encoding. With it you can:

-   [**Deploy smart contract**](/docs/smart-contract/deploying-a-smart-contract): Deploy audited contract from our library or your own smart contract.
-   [**Import existing contract**](/docs/smart-contract/importing-smart-contract): Import existing contract to view them on your dashboard and interact with them.
-   [**Interact with a smart contract**](/docs/smart-contract/interacting-with-a-smart-contract): Read state and interact with the smart contract previously deployed or imported.
