# Wallets


To **interact with blockchains**, you need a **wallet for signing transactions**. For automated signing, you need a back-end wallet. Starton provides a **[wallet on their KMS for testing](/docs/wallet/creating-a-wallet)**. In production, you can **[grant Starton access to a wallet in your KMS](/docs/wallet/connecting-aws-kms)**.
